<hr>
<p>	If you have a favorite item, just let me know! If you aren't sure what item fits your personality best, poogle Neocodex's item database. Boom just needs the name of the item. Everything else is movie magic!</p>

<h3>Choosing a Color:</h3>
<hr>
<p>	There are three options for this part. With the first two, either provide a hex code (#349afc) or a color name ("red").<br>
<ol>
<li>Choose your favorite color. Whether or not it's in the image, any color can make a good background.</li>
<li>Choose a color from the image. This method was used in many of our already existing icons.</li>
<li>Let the random number generator choose for you! Boom will grab six random numbers and make a cool new color for you.</li>
</ol></p>
