<hr>

<li>Choose your favorite color. Whether or not it's in the image, any color can make a good background.</li>
<li>Choose a color from the image. This method was used in many of our already existing icons.</li>
<li>Let the random number generator choose for you! Boom will grab six random numbers and make a cool new color for you.</li>
</ol></p>
